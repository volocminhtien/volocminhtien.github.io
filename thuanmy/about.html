About Us

Welcome to Thuan My

We are part of Vietnam's leading private
healthcare network,
Hoan My Medical Group.

Thuan My is dedicated to making high-quality healthcare accessible for everyone. Our network of hospitals and clinics are located in Binh Duong and Dong Nai provinces, as well as in Ho Chi Minh City, bringing essential medical services to communities in Vietnam.

We are committed to delivering comprehensive care with advanced medical technologies, ensuring that patients receive the best possible treatment at affordable prices.

Our dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and healthcare professionals stand at the forefront of fulfilling Vietnam's healthcare needs.
